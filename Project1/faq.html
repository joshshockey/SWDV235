Have Questions?

What is Throat Honey?

Some might argue that it is a cheeky nod to everyone's favorite lozenge, Ricola!(Well not everyone's, but you get the gist) There is some truth to this, but in reality it's a group of 4 guys that love to play fast, catchy punk rock songs. Pulling inspiration from the likes of bands such as Blink-182, Beastie Boys, and Green Day to name a few, we don't take ourselves too seriously and love kicking out the jams for anyone in ear's reach.

Do you have merch for sale?

We are still working on our first release, which will be coming out this summer so stay tuned. We do have t-shirts, buttons, and patches available at our shows though so get'em while they're hot!

Where can you see us play live?

We play at The Hive here in Boise all the time. Please check out their webpage and sign up for our newsletter for up to date info.
